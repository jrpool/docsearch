Electricity Conservation at Centerplex

Since the early 1990s, Centerplex has been saving electricity with:

  • Lighting fixture improvements
  • Lighting control improvements
  • Thermostat improvements
  • Insulation improvements
  • Glazing improvements
  • Outside-air control improvements
  • Electricity metering improvements
  • Temperature monitoring improvements
  • Airflow monitoring improvements

Further energy efficiencies are being introduced in 2004 with additional improvements in lighting fixtures.
